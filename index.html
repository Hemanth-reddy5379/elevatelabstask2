Phishing Email Analysis Report

Sample Email Details

  • Subject: Your Account Has Been Suspended – Immediate Action Required!
  • Sender Email: support@paypalsecurity.com
  • Email Spoofing Detected: Yes

Email Header Discrepancies

  • Return-Path differs from From address
  • Received IP not matching official domain IP

Suspicious Links or Attachments

  • Link Text: Verify Your Account → URL: http://malicious-site.ru/login.php (Mismatched)
  • Attachment: account_update_form.zip (Malicious)

Urgent or Threatening Language

  • Immediate action required
  • Your account will be permanently disabled

Spelling or Grammar Errors

  • spe ling
  • ski ls
  • Your acount has been supended

Summary of Phishing Traits

  • Spoofed email domain resembling PayPal
  • Mismatch between displayed and actual URL
  • Grammar and spelling errors
  • Urgent call to action and account threat
  • Malicious ZIP attachment
  • Discrepant email header routing

Interview Questions

  • How can you identify a spoofed email address?
  • What tools can you use to analyze email headers?
  • Why is hovering over links important in phishing detection?
  • What role does urgency play in phishing attacks?
  • Can phishing emails include correct logos or branding?
  • How do you handle a suspicious email in a corporate environment?

Learning Outcome

User understands phishing tactics including spoofed emails, header anomalies, malicious links, urgency manipulation, and grammar errors. Demonstrates practical skill in email threat analysis.
